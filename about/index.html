Overview:

Aerospace Technologies:

At Palmara we aim to incorporate automated aerospace solutions to provide robust and reliable imaging solutions for every industry.

Utilising a modular design, where features and functionality are built on top of a common platform, we aim to offer wide range imaging services to meet all types of needs, from high speed action shots to in depth multi-spectrum imaging.

Currently in the initial stages of start-up, our company is designing & developing the first version of its base platform that future models will be built on.

For more information, with regards to testing and development, please contact us via email.
