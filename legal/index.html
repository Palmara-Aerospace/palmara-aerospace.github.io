Trademarks & Registered Entities:

A trademark can be a word, phrase, or symbol that distinguishes the source of goods or services. It can also be the appearance of a product or its packaging, including shape, size, colour, texture, graphics, and appearance.

When using trademarks or other entities registered to Palmara Ltd. in publications that will be distributed in the United Kingdom, include the appropriate ™ or ® symbol on first use. For publications that will be distributed outside the United Kingdom, do not include trademark symbols. Instead use the appropriate trademark attribution notice, for example: "<name> is a registered entity / trademark of Palmara Ltd., registered in the United Kingdom".

Please note that names and trademarks associated with Palmara Ltd. are adjectives, and should not be made into verbs or converted into plural or possessive form.
